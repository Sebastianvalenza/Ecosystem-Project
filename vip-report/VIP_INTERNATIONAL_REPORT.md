## 5. Unification Strategy

### 5.1 Goals
1. **Single CRM Platform** — Migrate all 18 regional operations into one unified CRM by Q3 2026.
2. **Standardized Playbooks** — Roll out uniform escalation and service scripts globally.
3. **24/7 Follow-the-Sun Coverage** — Leverage time zone spread across NA, EU, AP, and MEA so at least one team is always active.
4. **Cross-Regional Backup** — Each agent is paired with a counterpart in another region for redundancy.
5. **Unified VIP Portal** — Single client-facing portal replacing the current 6 regional portals.

### 5.2 Follow-the-Sun Coverage Map

```
 UTC 00-06  → Asia-Pacific (AP team online)
 UTC 06-12  → Middle East & Europe (MEA + EU teams online)
 UTC 12-18  → Europe + Americas overlap (EU + NA/SA teams online)
 UTC 18-00  → Americas (NA + SA teams online)
```
